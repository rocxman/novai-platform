## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Overview

**NOVA AI Platform** is an enterprise-grade, AI-powered creative generation platform designed to democratize high-quality video and image production for creators, businesses, and developers across Southeast Asia. Built on Google's cutting-edge AI infrastructure (Gemini, Imagen, Veo), NOVA delivers cinematic-quality visual content from simple text prompts or images.

### Business Opportunity

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Target Market** | SEA (820M+ population) |
| **Market Size (AI Video)** | USD 554M (2024) → USD 4.7B (2030) |
| **CAGR** | 41.2% |
| **Revenue Target (Year 1)** | USD 500K ARR |
| **Break-even** | 12 months |

### Key Differentiators

1. **Google AI Native** — First-mover advantage with Veo 2, Imagen 3, Gemini 1.5 Pro
2. **Bilingual UX** — Bahasa Indonesia + English for SEA market penetration
3. **API-First** — Developer-friendly integration from day one
4. **Enterprise Security** — GDPR + UU PDP compliant with SOC 2 readiness

### Investment Required

- **Initial Infrastructure:** USD 2,000/month
- **Team:** 7 FTE (2 Backend, 2 Frontend, 1 DevOps, 1 UI/UX, 1 PM)
- **Timeline to MVP:** 3 months

### Success Criteria

| KPI | Target |
|-----|--------|
| Users (30 days post-launch) | 10,000 |
| Generations (Month 1) | 50,000 |
| Paid Conversion | 5% |
| MRR (Month 3) | USD 10,000 |
| NPS Score | > 40 |
